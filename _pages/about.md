Graft, the multilingual review of the Claremont colleges.

<span style = "color: #777777">接木, Greffe, Trenza, 접목, Enxerto — a campus publication celebrating linguistic diversity.</span>

Welcome to Graft, a literary review that publishes creative and scholarly writings in a variety of languages with English translations. Our mission is to foster a vibrant multilingual writing community at the Claremont Colleges, where different languages can thrive side by side.

Explore works of fiction, essays, poetry, and more in Chinese, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, and beyond. Join us in celebrating the beauty of languages and the creativity of our international voices.
